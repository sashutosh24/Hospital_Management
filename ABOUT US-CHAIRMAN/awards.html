Awards and Accolades

.

ISO Certification – 2006

Recertified in 2009 with ISO 9001:2008

NABH Accredited Hospital

In 2007 Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital became the first hospital in Mumbai and the Western region of India to receive the prestigious NABH Accreditation for hospitals and healthcare providers- an honor accorded to only eight hospitals in the country at the time. The hospital was reaccredited in 2010

NABL Accredited Hospital

Department of Laboratory Medicine at Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital was accredited by National Accreditation Board in the year 2008. The laboratory became the second in Mumbai to be accredited by NABH.

NABH For Blood Bank – 2011

Dr L H Hiranandani hospital Blood Bank received NABH accreditation in the year 2011. The Blood Bank became the fourth in Mumbai to be accredited by NABH.

Best Concrete Structure – 2006

Dr L H Hiranandani receives the best made concrete building healthcare award for the best in category nationally in the year 2006.

IMC Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award – 2008

Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital is recipient of the IMC Ramkrishna Bajaj National Quality Award for the year 2008.

International Asia Pacific Quality Award – 2009

The hospital has also received the prestigious International Asia Pacific Award (IAPQA) conferred by the Asia Pacific Quality Organization (APQO). This is for the first time in India that a hospital has won the award. (Award evaluation process is based on the Malcolm Baldridge criteria).The bottom line being this hospital is known to render quality healthcare..
