Radiology


Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital in collaboration with SRL Diagnostics Pvt. Ltd. offers a range of radiological services from Conventional X-Rays to state of the art Cardiac CT and whole body MRI, all under one roof. We ensure that the tests / Investigations are done to individual satisfaction in a highly professional ambience.

Facilities Available
• Digital system computerized X-Ray with complete dry camera
• High Resolution Dual focus X Ray Unit
• 2/3/4-Dimensional Sonography
• Fully Automated Mamography
• State of the art 128 slice Dual source Cardiac CT.
• State of the art MRI.1.5 Tesla.
• DEXA - High precision pencil beam technology
• State of the art OPG (Orthopantomogram), for Bone Density and Obesity.
