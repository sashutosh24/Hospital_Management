Gastrointestinal Surgery

At Dr L H Hiranandani hospital the department has 10 surgeons whose combined experiences provide comprehensive care for the diseases of the gastrointestinal tract (esophagus, stomach, small intestine and colon), pancreas and liver.

The hospital encourages the department to emphasize on a team approach, enlisting the cooperation of other departments in providing a multidisciplinary approach to patient care. The interaction provides a large number of specialists to provide the most advanced techniques in diagnosis and therapy.

Services Offered
Each of the surgeons has expertise in the field of minimal access surgery and some engage in vertical specializations as gastrointestinal malignancies, Bariatric Surgery and such like. Care pathways include a broad based multidisciplinary clinical program for malignant disorders of the gastrointestinal tract. The program offers evaluation and treatment for patients with cancer of the digestive tract and liver. The centre is also recognized by the government as a centre for liver transplant.
