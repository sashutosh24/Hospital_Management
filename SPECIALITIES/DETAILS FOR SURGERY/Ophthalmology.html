Ophthalmology


The centre for advanced and sophisticated eye care provides state of the art and most modern ophthalmic services for various eye conditions. We strive to maintain international standards in medical and surgical ophthalmic care.

Eye Center In Mumbai
Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital eye centre provides various specialized services in all fields of ophthalmology. These include

Cataract and Refractive Surgery
With advanced Phacoemulsification techniques using the state of the art equipment, the centre offers sutureless cataract surgery with Foldable Intraocular Lens Implantation including monofocal, multifocal aberration free IOLs and accommodative IOLs. YAG-Laser Capsulotomy for the treatment of after cataract.

Glaucoma detection using Tonometry (Noncontact, Applanation), Computerized Automated visual field testing, pachymetry, Gonioscopy and Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT).
Glaucoma treatment is done with laser (YAG laser iridotomy, selective laser trabeculoplasty), penetrating and non penetrating surgeries (Trabeculectomy with anti metabolites, NPDS and drainage valve surgeries for advanced and resistant glaucomas.)

Retina &Vitreous
Treatment of various retinopathies like Diabetic Retinopathy, Hypertensive Retinopathies, Central Serous Retinopathy, Venous Occlusions, Eales Disease and Retinopathy of prematurity using lasers with silt-lamp, Indirect Ophthalmoscopic laser delivery and Endolaser, Photodynamic Therapy(PDT) and injections of anti-VEGF agents(Avastin,Macugen,Lucentis)for neovascular membranes in Age related macular degeneration (ARMD). Advanced Vitreoretinal microsurgery with use of perflurocarbons, silicone oil and band buckle for complicated retinal detachment, complicated diabetic cases with hemorrhage, and retinal detachments for posterior segment trauma, epiretinal membranes, macular holes and submacular surgeries.

Cornea
• Detection and treatment of corneal disorders-like corneal infections, keratoconus, pterygium etc, 

• Pterygium surgery with auto-graft with amniotic membrane transplant

• Full fledged Corneal Transplant Programme

Squint & Orthoptic Service
Detection and surgical correction of Squint and Amblyopia treatment

Pediatric Ophthalmology
For diagnosis and treatment of various eye disorders including refractive errors and lazy eye treatment, Congenital Cataract, Retinopathy of prematurity(ROP) - Screening, laser treatment and Vitreoretinal surgeries

Uveitis & Ocular Inflammation
Diagnosis and treatment of primary and secondary inflammatory conditions of the eye

Orbit & Oculoplasty
Eyelid surgery for entropion, ectropin and ptosis, socket surgery and removal of orbital tumors

Neuro-Ophthalmology Service
Diagnosis and treatment of inter-related disorders of the eye and neurological

Operation Theatre
The operation theatre complex for ophthalmic surgeries under Local and General Anesthesia is equipped with state-of –the- art Ziess Microscope. A complete life support & monitoring equipment including cardiac monitors, pulse oximeter, defibrillator and arterial blood gas analysers is available in the theatre. Anesthetists are available round the clock for procedures.

Advanced Diagnostic Facilities:
IOL Master
This device is a non-contact device for performing accurate measurements of the eye and intraocular lens power calculations for patients undergoing cataract surgery

Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)
Cirrus OCT (Zeiss) is a new, noninvasive, noncontact, imaging technology which can produce very high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina. This technology can be used to help diagnose and thus treat a variety of retinal disorders including diabetic retinopathy, Age related macular degeneration (ARMD), Cystoid macular edema macular holes, Epiretinal membranes, central serous choriodopathy and optic disc pits. 
It also gives high resolution imaging of the optic nerve for early glaucoma detection. The new model of the machine we have also has an anterior segment module for imaging of cornea & anterior segment.

Fluroscein angiography & Fundus Photography
Fluroscein angiography is an important diagnostic tool to study the retinal vasculature. This involves an injection of fluroscein dye intravenously and then taking serial retinal photographs. This helps in diagnosing diseases of retina and choroid and also in evaluating the effect of treatment (medical or laser). The most common indications are diabetic retinopathy, ARMD and vascular occlusions of the retina.

YAG Laser
For treatment of narrow angle glaucomas, after cataract (PCO)

Green Laser
For various retinal conditions including diabetic retinopathy, vascular occlusions, myopic retinal degenerations, ROP among others

f. A & B scan – Ultrasonography
Ultrasound is an important non invasive diagnostic tool when visualization of the internal structure of the eyes if not possible due to dense cataract or blood. It enables us to assess the visual prognosis better and plan the surgical procedures accordingly. The three dimensional mode available in the machine is very useful in diagnosing mass lesions (tumors, etc) of the eye better.

Perimeter
This is the most important and advanced equipment useful in the diagnosis of glaucoma and certain neurological disorders. This is fully loaded machine along with software’s to assess the progression of glaucoma. We get to know the field of vision of the patient.

Auto Lensmeter
This is useful to calculate the present glassed power more accurately and instantly.

Visual Evoked Potential (VEP)
To assess optic nerve disorders in coordination with the neurology department

CT scan & MRI
Imaging of eye in case of Trauma, Intraocular foreign body, Orbital tumor, Thyroid Ophthalmopathy and optic nerve related disorders.
