Pulmonology


Pulmonology is a specialty that deals with the health of the lower respiratory tract.

Facilities
• Department has got the latest Pulmonary Function lab for performing various lung function tests
• ICU has got latest ventilators and Non-invasive for dealing with Respiratory emergencies. 
• Department has got Video Bronchoscope for performing diagnostic and therapeutic Bronchoscopy.

Services Offered
• The Department of Pulmonology is well equipped to provide state-of-the-art care for respiratory diseases like: Bronchial asthma, COPD, Respiratory tract infections- Tuberculosis, Pneumonias, Lung Cancer, Pleural Disease, Allergy and Sleep Disorders.

• The Department is fully equipped to deal with respiratory emergencies like exacerbation of Asthma, COPD, Haemoptysis, pulmonary embolisms and respiratory failure.

Speciality Clinic
Sleep Medicine is developed as a sub-specialty with active contribution of Pulmonologists and Neurologists, we have a dedicated sleep lab to diagnose and treat various sleep disorders.
