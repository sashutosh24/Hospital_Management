Cafeteria

.

At Dr RAR Hospital, you can visit two different cafeterias.

Ground floor cafeteria is run by the ISS catering services that also cater to the hospital.

The cafeteria caters for all forms of snacks and meals. On the ninth floor we have the famous coffee Day Express (Café Coffee day Chain) While you enjoy, coffee, tea, or any other beverage you also get a panoramic view of the Powai lake.
