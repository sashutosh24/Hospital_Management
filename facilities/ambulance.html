Ambulance Services

.

The hospital has a small fleet of very well equipped ambulances.

The basic task here is to retrieve emergency cases from the proximity areas of the hospital.

The ambulances are equipped with advanced life support systems and carry a doctor on board for cardiac and other life threatening emergencies.
