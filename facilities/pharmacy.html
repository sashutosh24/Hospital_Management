Pharmacy

.

The pharmacy store is located at the hospital basement whereas the day and night retail pharmacy is located at the ground floor. The ward pharmacy is strategically located on the sixth floor catering to the medicine and surgical requirements of patients admitted from fourth floor to twelfth floor. Our pharmacy is staffed only by registered pharmacist.
