Nursing - A Challenge

At Dr L H Hiranandani Hospital, we are pushing back the traditional boundaries of Nursing and rendering high quality care for our patients. 

Benchmarks
• High quality nursing care
• To treat all with dignity and respect.
• Enhance health for all through community interaction and Home Care.
• Emulate global standards in Nursing. 

Competencies
• The Nursing department is continually upgrading nursing skills through continuous training.
• High level of clinical knowledge and technical skill.
• Kindness and empathy.
• Systematic approach to individualized nursing care.

Home Care
• We provide quality support and preventive healthcare services at the patient’s place of residence, and assist the patient and family to maintain as normal a lifestyle as is possible.
• Qualified Nurses provide both routine and specialized nursing care.
